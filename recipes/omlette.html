Omlette

Description

It's a dish well known all across the world made with eggs.

I usually make it at home during breakfast.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • Oil
  • onions
  • chilli flakes
  • salt

Steps

  1. spray oil on a pan
  2. cut some onions and add salt anc chilli flakes to it
  3. Break two eggs in the onions
  4. Beat the mixture
  5. Pour it in the pan and fry it
